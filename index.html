Mission

Limit transmission of the COVID-19 virus while enabling protesters to gather at in person events to enact their right to protest in support of #blacklivesmatter. You can follow our hashtag on various social media with #ppeforsafeprotesting.

Who we are

We are a small group of individuals based in the Oakland, CA area who support the #BLM #blacklivesmatter protests and also support measures to limit the spread of COVID-19. Given the impracticality of social distancing while protesting in person, we would like to help support the cause by distributing PPE protesters can use (like cloth masks/face shields) for usage while protests continue. We are NOT healthcare professionals and recommend visiting the CDC website for guidance on PPE and usage of it.

Contact Us

You can contact us by emailing us here. We are looking for feedback, volunteers who are interested in also distributing PPE to protest sites and recommendations by health care professionals on information to cite and further reading.
